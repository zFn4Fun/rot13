ROT13 Ecoder/Decoder

ROT13 or ROT-13 (short for ROTate 13) is a simple letter substitution cipher that replaces a letter with the letter 13 letters after it in the basic Latin alphabet.

A major advantage of ROT13 is that it is its own inverse, meaning the same algorithm applies for both encoding and decoding.

You can read more about ROT13 on wikipedia.
